Objective

The goal of this project will be to show trends of mobile processors, growth of companies behind them, sales of models and how mobile processors are overall more efficient in power and costs and show why they will lead in the tech market. I will also be comparing them to desktop processors.

Benchscores

As it can be seen below, Mobile Processors rivals and even surpasses the Single-Core Benchscores of traditonal desktop processors.

Traditonal desktop processors are way ahead in terms of Multi-Core Benchscores compared to Mobile, But these are mostly server processors with many more cores compared to mobile. For most of us, We do not need such computational power for our daily computer tasks.

Mobile processors are smaller, more efficient, and even be more cost effective in some areas. Especially when considering that today’s daily average computing does not need the use of multiple cores in order to compute text editors, web browsing, video streaming, etc. In theory, computers with mobile processors should be more power efficient, meaning a longer battery life and more cost effective overall. With the current direction of progress, Mobile Processors will soon be standard, and as developers, we must prepare and maybe consider change direction of development to keep mobile in future plans.

Tech Companies

One of the ways that technology becomes standard is market domination. Such as how Microsoft dominated the tech industries in the early 80s and 90s. That is why Windows OS and many Windows software are now standard. But through the following graphs, we’ll see how much the market has changed. And how Apple is leading after 2020.

Below is all of the stock data put together. Microsoft had market domination for a while until in the 2010s, when Apple became a fierce competitor. We can see Apple and Amd are growing in the late 2010s, due to their partnership with graphics chips. As soon as the Apple M1 Chip was announced in late 2020, AMD has fallen a bit, Most likely due to investors predicting that Apple plans to become fully independent in chip manufacturing.

Knowing where the market is going is important for developers. We can use it to better predict what will be the new industry standard so our work does get left behind. As an inspiring software developer, I will be looking into developing under the M1 platform as I believe it will soon become the tech industry standard.
